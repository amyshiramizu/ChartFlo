Upload orders → Practice Fusion

Loads the most recent orders Chart Flo sent to the extension (or paste your own). Edit the list, then push them into the active Practice Fusion encounter's Plan field, or stage them for the next fax compose page.

How it works

Push to active encounter finds the Plan / Orders field in the open Practice Fusion chart-note and inserts the formatted order list.
Stage for fax page stores these orders so the next time you open a PF fax compose screen, the Chart Flo banner offers a one-click "Fill fax" button to auto-populate recipient, subject, and message body.

Settings
